Community Reviews

Rate Your HOA Management Company

Management companies work for the board — but the board works for you. Share your experience to hold management companies accountable and help other homeowners make informed decisions.

Review Policy: Reviews are submitted by HOA members and reflect their personal experiences. We do not verify individual reviews. We reserve the right to remove content that is defamatory, includes personal information about private individuals, or violates our terms. Always conduct your own research before making decisions about your HOA.

Submit a Review

Share Your Experience

Your review helps other homeowners know what to expect from HOA management companies in their area. Be specific, be factual, and focus on behaviors you personally experienced.

Tips for a Helpful Review
  • Be specific — vague reviews are less useful than specific ones
  • Include dates and timeframes when relevant
  • Focus on the management company's behavior, not the board's
  • Describe both positives and negatives if applicable
  • Do not include personal information about private individuals
  • Stick to your own direct experience
Why Reviews Matter

HOA management companies are hired by boards, not by members — yet members live with the consequences every day. Reviews create accountability where there was none, give future members information before they buy into a community, and give honest companies a way to stand apart from bad actors.
