Our Story

Built by someone who lost everything to an HOA dispute.

A Hawaii homeowner lost their home in a devastating brush fire. In the aftermath of the disaster, when the family turned to their HOA for support in navigating the insurance process, the board denied their claim — citing provisions buried in the CC&Rs that the homeowner had never been clearly informed of. The family went through months of mediation that went nowhere. Determined to change the system, they took a bill to the Hawaii state legislature that would have required HOAs to provide plain-language disclosure of insurance coverage responsibilities to every member. The bill failed — killed in committee after HOA management companies hired lobbyists to oppose it.

That experience revealed how little most HOA members know about their rights — and how much the system is stacked against them. This site exists to change that.
